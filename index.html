Book Description «Asylum»

The sinister secrets of the old school come to life again! For all fans of the "House of Strange Children". County old house... Thick gloomy walls, drowning out all the screams. Dan and his friends have strange and scary dreams about, what happened to them last summer at New Hampshire College. To get rid of them, there is only one way out — to return to the same place and face your fears. When each of them receives a frightening photo with the inscription "It's not over with you", the guys understand that it's time to act. They need to find the key to solving an old mystery.

«Asylum» - a brief plot

Old walls keep a lot of creepy secrets… Sixteen-year-old Dan Crawford did not even know that the summer student dormitory once housed a hospital for mentally ill criminals. Exploring the secret corners of the former psychiatric hospital, Dan will face dark secrets, strangely connected to his past…
